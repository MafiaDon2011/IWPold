The Course

Participants will:

  • Gain new ways to implement all phases of the writing process.
  • Learn new strategies for raising achievement through content-rich writing.
  • Improve their personal and professional writing.
  • Reflect on their own experiences as writers and apply them in their own classrooms.
  • Learn about frameworks for seeking and evaluating feedback on writing.
  • Incorporate cultural experiences and global issues into writing instruction.
  • Explore assessment tools, including rubrics, revision notes, and self-assessment.
  • Share ideas with teachers from other schools.
